Leonardo Furlan () is an italian artist born in 1998; he studied painting and graduated at Venice Academy of Fine Arts in 2024.

In his paintings, that he makes like iconic apparition, he brings together multiple interests he’s into, in multiple ways. The paradox of inconcistency and power of originality and banality is for sure a central theme: Who and when someone can tell if a work is more original than other ones? Why and how is it important? Leonardo reflects about individual and common life experiences, on the way they, inseparable, create a shared but at the same time personal imaginary.

Born on March 4th 1998.
